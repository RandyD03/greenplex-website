    ANTI-AGING COLLECTION

    About The Collection:

    Aging skin is typically dull and dry due to your skin loosing hyaluronic acid and collagen with age. We suggest you to use Vitamin C facial serum to start your skin care routine. Vitamin C facial serum is rich in Vitamin C and hyaluronic acid. They are both necessary ingredients to form collagen in your skin. It helps to boost collagen formation and replenish water to your dry skin. It will smooth fine lines and wrinkles. For the eye area, use the Anti-aging Eye Complex, which is rich in Vitamin C, amino acid and hyaluronic acid. It reduce fine lines and wrinkles around eye area, tightening and helps to lighten dark circles. Finally use the Vitamin C Facial Lotion to moisturize your skin and lock water. We suggest you to use Avocado Hydrating Overnight sleeping mask twice to three times per week before you go to bed. keep it on during your sleeping and wash it off the next morning. Avocado hydrating Overnight Sleeping Mask moisturizes your skin and lock the water during your sleep. The next morning you will find your skin is suffle and hydrated.

    Target Skin-Type:

    Aging skin

    Application Steps:

    After washing your face, apply the Vitamin C Eye Complex around your eye area. Then apply the rest of the skin care products in the sequence of Vitamin C Anti-aging Facial toner (Optional), Vitamin C Facial Serum and Vitamin C Anti-aging Facial lotion. Please note Anti-aging Vitamin C Facial Lotion can be used for both day lotion and might lotion. We suggest you to use the Avocado Overnight Sleeping Mask twice or three times per week to get the best result. When using the Avocado Overnight Sleeping Mask, just wash you face, then apply the Facial Serum before applying the Overnight Sleeping Mask.
