    BRIGHTENING COLLECTION

    About The Collection:

    Use all 3 products in this collection to get the best result to lighten your skin tone and brightening your dull skin. Tamarind facial toner is rich in hyaluronic acid from the natural source, tamarind. As a natural "skin bleach", Tamarind facial toner opens up the pores and prepares your skin to absorb the facial serum and facial cream. Vitamin C facial serum is loaded with vitamin C and hyaluronic acid, which dramatically lightens your skin tone. Avocado facial cream further moisturizes your skin and locks in moisture.

    Target Skin-Type:

    Dull skin / Dark skin tone

    Application Steps:

    After washing your face, apply Tamarind Facial Toner, followed by Vitamin C Facial Serum and Avocado Facial Cream

    Tamarind Facial Toner

    Balance PH. Makes it easier for your skin to absorb the facial serum and facial cream. Helps to lighten your skin tone

    Vitamin C Facial Serum

    No Scent. With Hyaluronic Acid. Helps to lighten sun spots. Brightening, Anti-aging and boosts collagen formation

    Radiant Avocado Facial Cream

    With hyaluronic acid. Hydrating, moisturizing and brightening. Prevents fine lines and wrinkles
